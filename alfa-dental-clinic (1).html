About Alfa Dental Clinic

Local roots, city-level treatment standards.

Why we exist

Most advanced aligner and whitening treatment in Tamil Nadu is concentrated in Chennai's city core. Patients from Ramanathapuram and surrounding areas were driving hours for consultations. Alfa Dental Clinic was built to close that gap — same treatment quality, without the commute.

How we treat

Every case starts with a full diagnostic consultation before any treatment plan is proposed. We don't upsell procedures patients don't need — pricing and timelines are explained upfront, in plain terms, before you commit to anything.

Meet the doctor

The clinician behind your treatment plan.

Dr

Dr. A. Alfa Ranjith, BDS

Chief Dental Surgeon & Clinic Founder

Dr. Ranjith founded Alfa Dental Clinic with a focus on orthodontic and cosmetic dentistry — specifically clear aligner therapy and Invisalign treatment planning. Every case is personally reviewed and mapped before any aligner sequence is ordered.

The clinic treats patients across Ramanathapuram and the wider Chalai Bazar region, with a client base that includes multi-year family patients as well as short-course cosmetic cases like whitening ahead of events.
