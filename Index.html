Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

How many sessions are required for laser hair removal?

Typically, 6-8 sessions are recommended for optimal results, spaced 4-6 weeks apart.

Is microneedling painful?

A topical anesthetic cream is applied before the procedure to minimize discomfort.

What is the recovery time for hair transplantation?

Initial recovery takes about 1-2 weeks, with full results visible in 6-12 months.

Are cosmetic surgeries safe?

All our cosmetic surgeries are performed by experienced, certified surgeons in a sterile environment, ensuring utmost safety.

How long do skin tightening results last?

Results can last from 1-3 years, depending on the treatment type and individual factors.
